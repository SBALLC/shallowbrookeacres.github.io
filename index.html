About Us

Welcome to Shallowbrook Acres, a family-run farm in Clarke County, Virginia. We take pride in producing high-quality, all-natural products using sustainable and ethical farming practices. From pasture-raised meats to handcrafted skincare, everything we make is designed to bring the best of the farm to your home.

Our Products

Certified Angus Beef

We proudly raise **Certified Angus Beef**, known for its rich flavor and superior marbling. Whether you’re looking for ribeye, filet mignon, or hearty ground beef, our farm-to-table cuts are processed with the highest standards of quality and care.

Bacon & Ham Steaks

Our **nitrate-free, cold-smoked bacon** comes in regular and peppered varieties, along with thick-cut ham steaks that bring rich, smoky flavor to your breakfast or dinner table.

Homemade Apple Butter

Slow-cooked over an **open fire** just like our ancestors did, our apple butter is rich, smooth, and packed with deep, caramelized flavor.
