About Us

Welcome to Shallowbrook Acres, your local source for high-quality farm products in Clarke County, Virginia. We are dedicated to providing the community with fresh, ethically raised farm meat, custom-crafted sausage, and a variety of homemade goods.

At Shallowbrook Acres, we believe in using every part of what we raise, which is why we offer beef tallow moisturizers and creams, hand-poured tallow candles, and other handcrafted products made with care. Our farm is built on a passion for sustainable practices, traditional craftsmanship, and a commitment to offering natural, wholesome products to our neighbors.

We take pride in serving the local area with farm-to-table freshness and a personal touch. Whether you're looking for premium meats, custom sausage blends, or nourishing skincare products, Shallowbrook Acres is here for you.

Thank you for supporting small farms and choosing local!
