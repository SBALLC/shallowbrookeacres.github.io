About Us

Welcome to Shallowbrook Acres, a family-run farm in Clarke County, Virginia, dedicated to producing high-quality, all-natural products. We believe in sustainable farming, ethical practices, and bringing the freshest products directly to our local community.

From pasture-raised meats to handcrafted tallow-based skincare, everything we make is rooted in our commitment to quality and tradition. Whether you're looking for farm-fresh food or all-natural home goods, we're here to serve you with the best nature has to offer.

Our Products

Farm-Fresh Meats

We raise our livestock with care, ensuring they live on open pastures and receive natural feed. Our beef and pork are free from added hormones and antibiotics, resulting in superior taste and quality. Whether you're looking for steaks, roasts, bacon, or ground beef, we’ve got something for every meal.

Custom Sausage

Our handcrafted sausages are made with locally sourced ingredients and seasoned to perfection. Choose from traditional flavors or try something unique—like our seasonal apple-maple sausage or spicy jalapeño cheddar.

Beef Tallow Skincare

Our beef tallow moisturizers and creams are perfect for hydrating and nourishing the skin naturally. Tallow has been used for centuries as a powerful, non-toxic skincare ingredient, offering deep hydration without artificial chemicals.

Tallow Candles

Made with pure beef tallow, our candles provide a clean, slow burn and a subtle, natural scent. Unlike paraffin candles, they don’t release harmful chemicals into the air, making them a healthier choice for your home.
