About Us

Welcome to Shallowbrooke Acres, a small farm in Clarke County, Virginia, providing fresh meats, natural skincare, and handmade goods for our local community.

Beef

Pasture-raised, hormone-free beef cuts available in bulk or by the cut.

Sausage

Custom-blended sausage made fresh with herbs and spices. Perfect for breakfast or grilling.

Bacon

Thick-cut, naturally smoked bacon from our pasture-raised pork.

Tallow Skin Balm

Handcrafted beef tallow balm that deeply nourishes and protects your skin.

Soy Candles

Clean-burning soy candles in a variety of scents. Handmade in small batches.
