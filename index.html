About Us

Welcome to Shallowbrook Acres, a family-run farm in Clarke County, Virginia, dedicated to providing high-quality, all-natural products. Our focus is on sustainable farming, ethical practices, and bringing the freshest farm-raised meats and handcrafted goods to the community. Every cut of meat, every jar of apple butter, and every handmade product is crafted with care and tradition, ensuring you get the best from our farm to your table.

Our Products

Certified Angus Beef

Our farm is proud to offer Certified Angus Beef, known for its superior marbling, tenderness, and rich flavor. Raised with care, our beef delivers top-tier quality that elevates any meal, whether you're preparing a thick, juicy steak, a slow-cooked roast, or the perfect burger. We ensure every cut meets high standards of quality, so you can enjoy beef that’s truly exceptional.

Nitrate-Free Smoked Bacon & Ham Steaks

Our bacon and ham steaks are cold-smoked to perfection using traditional methods, just as it should be. Available in regular and peppered varieties, our nitrate-free bacon brings out a natural, deep-smoked flavor that’s perfect for breakfast or adding a rich touch to your favorite dishes. Our thick-cut ham steaks are just as flavorful—savory, smoky, and ready for your skillet or grill.

Custom Pork & Lamb Sausage

We take pride in crafting high-quality, custom sausages using farm-raised pork and lamb, blended with the perfect balance of seasonings. Whether you prefer a classic breakfast sausage, a rich and smoky link, or something bold with a spicy kick, we can create a flavor that fits your taste. No fillers, no unnecessary additives—just fresh, real ingredients that bring out the best in every bite.

Beef Tallow Skincare

Beef tallow has been used for centuries as a natural, nourishing moisturizer. Our handcrafted skincare products harness the power of pure beef tallow, providing deep hydration and essential nutrients that repair and protect your skin. Packed with vitamins A, D, E, and K, our tallow balms and creams are perfect for dry, chapped skin, offering a simple yet effective way to nourish your body naturally.

Tallow Candles

Our long-burning tallow candles are crafted using pure beef tallow for a natural, clean-burning glow. Whether you're looking to create a cozy atmosphere or enjoy a gentle, natural scent, our handcrafted candles bring warmth and tradition into your home.

Homemade Apple Butter

We make our apple butter the way our ancestors did—slow-cooked over an open fire, carefully stirred until it reaches rich, caramelized perfection. Using only the finest locally sourced apples and a warm blend of spices, this traditional spread brings a deep, authentic flavor that’s simply unbeatable.
